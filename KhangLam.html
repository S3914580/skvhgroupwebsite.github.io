This helps me to be prepared in many situations. Besides that. ease and the spirit of discovering new things helps me handle projects in a way that is not stereotypical, through which I can find novel but effective solutions. However, I am considered to be quite competitive, competition is good to keep me motivated, but too fierce competition can lead to counterproductive effects.
